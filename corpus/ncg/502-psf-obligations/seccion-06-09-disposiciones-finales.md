## VI — Operational Capacity
**NCG anchor:** N° 502, Sección VI
**Implements:** Ley 21.521 Art. 5 (registration / operational readiness) and Art. 7 (authorization to provide services — operational-capacity condition)
**Tags:** operational-capacity, authorization, infrastructure, stress-testing

### Plain-English text

Those who provide the alternative trading system (sistema alternativo de transacción), order routing (enrutamiento de órdenes), and intermediation and custody of financial instruments (intermediación y custodia de instrumentos financieros) services must have the operational capacity (capacidad operacional) to support the processing of the transactions or operations carried out through their systems or infrastructure, in accordance with what is established in Article 7 of Ley N° 21.521.

To evidence that operational capacity, the applicant, at the time of requesting authorization for the services, must declare — identifying itself by means of the electronic mechanism provided for those purposes on the Commission (CMF)'s website — that its systems and infrastructure will be in a position to process the projected transactions or operations once it has the Commission (CMF)'s authorization to carry out the service, and those estimated for the following three years. For those purposes, it must attach a description of the estimates made regarding the current and future capacity of its systems and infrastructure by type of service, expressing the capacity thereof as the maximum number of transactions or operations that may be carried out per unit of time and reporting the functional testing performed to verify that capacity, including the stress tests (pruebas de stress o tensión) carried out to determine the headroom with respect to expected demand and the procedures defined to monitor and adjust the operational capacity. For entities classified in Block 2 and Block 3 referred to in Section IV of this regulation, they must additionally attach a certification issued by a specialized third party in accordance with internationally accepted standards or best practices (estándares o buenas prácticas internacionales de común aceptación), which pronounces on the reliability and sufficiency of the tests performed by the entity, as well as their results.

### Original Spanish

> "VI. CAPACIDAD OPERACIONAL
>
> Quienes presten el servicio de sistema alternativo de transacción, enrutamiento de órdenes, intermediación y custodia de instrumentos financieros deberán contar con la capacidad operacional para soportar el procesamiento de las operaciones o transacciones que mediante sus sistemas o infraestructura se realicen conforme a lo establecido en el artículo 7 de la Ley N°21.521.
>
> Para acreditar dicha capacidad operacional el solicitante, al momento de requerir la autorización de los servicios, deberá declarar, identificándose mediante el mecanismo electrónico dispuesto para esos efectos en el sitio en Internet de la Comisión, que sus sistemas e infraestructura estarán en condiciones de procesar las operaciones o transacciones proyectadas una vez que cuente con la autorización de la Comisión para llevar a cabo el servicio y las estimadas para los próximos tres años. Para esos efectos deberá acompañar una descripción de las estimaciones realizadas sobre la capacidad actual y futura de sus sistemas e infraestructura por tipo de servicio, expresando la capacidad de los mismos como número máximo de operaciones o transacciones que se podrán realizar por unidad de tiempo y dando cuenta de las pruebas de funcionamiento realizadas para verificar dicha capacidad, incluyendo las pruebas de stress o tensión que se hubieren efectuado para determinar la holgura respecto a la demanda esperada y los procedimientos definidos para monitorear y ajustar la capacidad operacional. Tratándose de entidades clasificadas en el Bloque 2 y 3 al que se refiere la Sección IV de esta normativa, además deberán acompañar una certificación efectuada por un tercero especializado conforme a estándares o buenas prácticas internacionales de común aceptación, que se pronuncie respecto de la confiabilidad y suficiencia de las pruebas realizadas por la entidad, así como también de sus resultados."

## VII — Inherent Activities
**NCG anchor:** N° 502, Sección VII
**Implements:** Ley 21.521 Art. 5 (scope of activities inherent to regulated services — permitted without separate authorization as complementary activity)
**Tags:** inherent-activities, permitted-activities, complementary-activity, scope

### Plain-English text

For the purposes of Article 5 of Ley Fintec (Ley 21.521), the following activities are deemed inherent (inherentes) to the activity regulated by law and may therefore be carried out without requiring authorization as a complementary activity (actividad complementaria):

a) Carrying out foreign-exchange transactions (operaciones de cambio de divisas), for those who have the intermediation and custody of financial instruments activity authorized.

b) Tax advisory (asesoría tributaria) and financial planning (planificación financiera), and studies related to financial, micro-economic, or macro-economic matters, for those who have the investment advisory activity authorized.

c) Generation, commercialization, and dissemination of reports containing credit or commercial information, for those who have the credit advisory activity authorized.

d) Collection and exercise of rights arising from the intermediation or custody of financial instruments, for those who have either of those services authorized.

e) Commercialization and development of technological tools for the provision of financial services or support for the same, to the extent they are related to the services authorized to the entity.

f) Investment of own capital (capital propio) in financial instruments and securities.

g) Investment-limit control for clients, for those who have the intermediation or custody of financial instruments activity authorized.

h) Referring clients or acting as agents (mandatarios) for the sole reception of client orders from third parties authorized in Chile or abroad to act as intermediaries, for those who have the order routing and investment advisory activity authorized.

i) Channelling orders to domestic or international entities, for those who have the order routing activity authorized.

j) Compilation, processing, communication, and processing of data on financial obligations and commercial reports, for those who have the credit advisory activity authorized.

> **TN:** Item j) reproduces the PDF's apparent double use of "tratamiento" and "procesamiento" in the same sentence ("Compilación, tratamiento, comunicación y procesamiento de datos…"). Both are rendered as stated; "tratamiento" → "processing" and "procesamiento" → "processing" are near-synonyms in context, but the Spanish distinguishes them. The English is rendered faithfully following the PDF.

k) Intermediation of publicly offered securities (valores de oferta pública) exempt from registration in the registries kept by the Commission (CMF), for those who have the intermediation of financial instruments activity authorized.

l) Channelling of orders for the purchase or sale of publicly offered securities exempt from registration in the registries kept by the Commission (CMF), for those who have the order routing activity authorized.

m) Custody of publicly offered securities exempt from registration in the registries kept by the Commission (CMF), for those who have the custody of financial instruments activity authorized.

The foregoing is without prejudice to the complementary activities that this Commission (CMF) may authorize by regulation (normativa).

## IX — Entry into Force
**NCG anchor:** N° 502, Sección IX
**Implements:** n/a (effective-date clause)
**Tags:** vigencia, entry-into-force, transitional-provisions, investment-advisory

### Plain-English text

This regulation enters into force (entra en vigor) as of 3 February 2024.

Accordingly, pursuant to Article 2 of the transitory provisions (disposiciones transitorias) of the Law, providers of crowdfunding platform (plataforma de financiamiento colectivo), alternative trading system (sistema alternativo de transacción), credit advisory (asesoría crediticia), custody of financial instruments (custodia de instrumentos financieros), order routing (enrutamiento de órdenes), and intermediation of financial instruments (intermediación de instrumentos financieros) services must submit the respective applications for registration and authorization for the activities they were carrying out as of the date of entry into force of this regulation before 3 February 2025. It is noted that registration alone does not entitle the relevant entity to provide the services it intends to carry out, which is why it is the responsibility of each entity to procure the authorization for the services it wishes to provide at the same time as its registration, making both applications in parallel and submitting the supporting documents for both processes in a timely manner.

Service providers referred to in the second paragraph of this section that do not make the applications to this Commission (CMF) before 3 February 2025, or that have done so within that period but whose application is deemed abandoned (declarada abandonada) for failure to remedy the observations formulated within the period established by the Commission (CMF) or is rejected by it, must refrain from continuing to provide their services for the purpose of entering into new transactions and must carry out only those acts tending toward the conclusion of the transactions regulated by Ley N° 21.521 contracted before that date. In the case of investment advisory (asesoría de inversión) service providers, that deadline shall continue to be the one established in the final paragraph of the entry-into-force section of General Regulation N° 494, that is, before 3 February 2024.

Natural and legal persons (personas naturales y jurídicas) that, as of the date of entry into force of this General Regulation, are registered in the Registry of Financial-Service Providers (Registro de Prestadores de Servicios Financieros) must comply with this regulation and submit to this Commission (CMF) the supporting documents for registration and authorization of the investment advisory service before 3 August 2024, through the official channel for communication and submission of information between the Commission (CMF) and its supervised entities. If that deadline passes without the required supporting documents having been submitted, the Commission (CMF) may proceed with the revocation of the respective authorization and cancellation from the Registry. Those persons may continue providing the services referred to in the second paragraph of this section that they were carrying out as of the date of entry into force of this regulation, without prejudice that they must submit the respective authorization applications before 3 February 2025 and shall be subject to what is provided in the preceding paragraph with respect to those services.

Pending applications for registration in the Registry of Financial-Service Providers and for authorization of the investment advisory service that are in process as of the date of issue of this regulation shall be without effect (quedarán sin efecto), and the entities must refrain from providing the investment advisory service for as long as they do not present a new registration and authorization application. In order to continue providing that service while both applications are being processed, they must resubmit them no later than 3 February 2024. If presented after that deadline, they may not provide the investment advisory service while the Commission (CMF) has not granted the respective authorization. Once the procedure is declared abandoned for failure to remedy the observations formulated by the Commission (CMF) within the period established for that purpose, or the application is rejected, the entity may not continue providing the advisory service.

> **TN:** "Ley Fintec" used in §VII is the informal name for Ley N° 21.521 (Ley para Promover la Competencia e Inclusión Financiera a través de la Innovación y Tecnología en la Prestación de Servicios Financieros). The full formal name is used throughout the plain-English translations; "Ley Fintec" is preserved in Spanish quotations.

> "IX. VIGENCIA
>
> La presente normativa entra en vigor a contar del 3 de febrero de 2024.
>
> Por lo anterior, conforme lo dispuesto en el artículo segundo de las disposiciones transitorias de la Ley, los prestadores de los servicios de plataforma de financiamiento colectivo, sistemas alternativos de transacción, asesoría crediticia, custodia de instrumentos financieros, enrutamiento de órdenes e intermediación de instrumentos financieros deberán presentar las respectivas solicitudes de inscripción y autorización para las actividades que estuvieren realizando a la fecha de entrada en vigencia de esta normativa antes del 3 de febrero de 2025. En el caso de los prestadores del servicio de asesoría de inversión dicho plazo seguirá siendo el establecido en el inciso final de la sección de vigencia de la Norma de Carácter General N°494, esto es, antes del 3 de febrero de 2024.

Dichas personas podrán seguir prestando los servicios a los que se refiere el segundo párrafo de esta sección que estuvieren realizando a la fecha de entrada en vigencia de esta normativa, sin perjuicio de que deberán remitir las respectivas solicitudes de autorización antes del 3 de febrero de 2025 y quedarán sujetos a lo dispuesto en el inciso precedente respecto de tales servicios.
>
> Las solicitudes de inscripción en el Registro de Prestadores de Servicios Financieros y de autorización del servicio de asesoría de inversión que estén pendientes de tramitación a la fecha de emisión de esta normativa quedarán sin efecto, debiendo las entidades abstenerse de prestar el servicio de asesoría de inversión en tanto no presenten una nueva solicitud de inscripción y de autorización. Para poder continuar prestando dicho servicio mientras se tramitan ambas solicitudes, deberán ingresarlas nuevamente a más tardar el 3 de febrero de 2024. En caso que se presenten con posterioridad a dicho plazo no podrán prestar el servicio de asesoría de inversión mientras la Comisión no hubiere conferido la respectiva autorización. Declarado abandonado el procedimiento por no haberse subsanado las observaciones formuladas por la Comisión dentro del plazo establecido por ésta para ello, o rechazada la solicitud, la entidad no podrá seguir prestando el servicio de asesoría."
