# NCG 503 — Exigencias de Idoneidad (Fit-and-Proper / Knowledge Accreditation)

**Issued:** 2024-01-12 by the Comisión para el Mercado Financiero (CMF). **In force:** 2025-01-13.
**Implements:** Ley 18.045 arts. 26, 28; Ley 20.712 arts. 8, 41, 98; Ley 19.220 arts. 7, 8; **Ley 21.521 (Ley Fintec) arts. 7, 9**.
**Derogates:** NCG N° 412.
**Source PDF:** `sources/ncg_503_2024.pdf`.

NCG 503 establishes the requirements to accredit the suitability (idoneidad) and
knowledge that natural persons must have to perform defined functions at regulated
entities (broker-dealers, securities agents, general fund managers, portfolio
managers, investment advisers, and others): the accreditation mechanism, the
functional categories that must accredit, temporary-exception conditions,
substitution by international accreditation, and the public accreditation registry.

Of its parent laws, only Ley 21.521 is in this corpus; cross-reference edges bridge
arts. 7 and 9. The other parent laws are cited textually in each section's
`Implements` line.
