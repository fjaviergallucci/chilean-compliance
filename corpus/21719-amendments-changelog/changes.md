## Notes

- The verb used in the table above for items 9 and 11 is `(multi-part)` because these are umbrella items containing multiple sub-amendments (a, b.i, b.ii, c, etc.).
- Items 5 and its sub-items (5.uno, 5.dos, 5.tres, 5.cuatro, 5.cinco) all touch Art. 2°. Item 5 is the umbrella entry; the sub-items are listed separately for completeness.
- All amendments listed above enter into force on **2026-12-01**, the effective date of Ley 21.719.
